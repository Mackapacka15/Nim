Welcome to Nim!
Nim is a simple strategy game you can play with your friends. This is a version where you can play if you don't have a opponent to play against. Here you can play against 2 different opponents width 2 different playstyles.

Rules:

The object of the game is to make the opponent take the last stick left in the pile. The ammount of sticks will be randomized in the beginning of the game. You can take 1, 2 or 3 sticks from the pile and you can't skipp a turn.
